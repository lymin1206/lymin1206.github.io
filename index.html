Short Biography

Hi all, I am Lie Meng. I obtained my PhD degree from Engineering faculty of University Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS) in 2018. Previously, I received my bachelor degree from UNIMAS in 2012.

Research interests

My research interests include: Fuzzy systems, soft computing, pattern recognition, evolutionary computation.
